Aim

At the heart of our mission lies a profound desire to kindle the spirit of adventure in every traveler, igniting within them the flames of unforgettable experiences that resonate for a lifetime.

We firmly believe that travel transcends mere escapism; it is a transformative journey that enriches our souls, broadens our perspectives, and fosters profound connections with the diverse tapestry of our world and its people.We are committed to responsible tourism and believe that travel should be sustainable and socially responsible. We promote ethical and eco-friendly travel practices, supporting local communities and preserving the natural beauty of our country.

Values

  1. Authenticity: We believe in promoting authentic cultural experiences and respecting the traditions and customs of the places we visit.
  2. Sustainability: We strive to be responsible travelers and promote sustainable tourism practices that protect the environment and support local communities.
  3. Service-oriented: Our customers are at the center of everything we do. We are committed to providing excellent customer service and creating personalized experiences that meet their unique needs and preferences.
  4. Transparency: We believe in being transparent and honest in our communications, pricing, and business practices.

Cradle of Humanity

Ardi (ARA-VP-6/500) is the designation of the fossilized skeletal remains of an Ardipithecus ramidus, thought to be an early human-like female anthropoid 4.4 million years old. It is the most complete early hominid specimen, with most of the skull, teeth, pelvis, hands and feet,[1] more complete than the previously known Australopithecus afarensis specimen called "Lucy". In all, 125 different pieces of fossilized bone were found.[2]
